    Yura Jeong was born in 2001, Korea. She studied at the Seoul National University of science and technology, where she received her BFA in painting. Yura moved to Germany in 2025 to study classical music. She has exhibited in galleries in New York and Berlin and has had solo exhibitions in Seoul.
